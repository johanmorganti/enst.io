Title : Resource Allocation for the Third Generation Cellular Systems

Abstract :

The work is conducted according to the proposed objectives for the UMTS (Universal Mobile Telecommunications System) radio access system. Our work is concerned with the definition, validation and performance evaluation of radio resource allocation algorithms. We investigate a dynamic channel allocation algorithm. Channel assignement algorithm decides the allocation of radio resources to any cell and to any call for a given instant. Requirements on channel assignement are the spectrum efficiency allocation of radio resources to the various radio bearer services and to provide a high level of flexibility to adapt to changes in the radio environment and traffic fluctuations. The interactions with algorithms for call admission control, and link quality (power control) would be investigated.
