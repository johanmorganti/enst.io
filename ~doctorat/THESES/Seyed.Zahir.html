Codage combine source-canal : Protection hierarchique



Auteur : Seyed Zahir

Thèse de l'ENST

Département Communications



Résumé :

The objective of combined source-channel coding is to include both the source and the channel coding (and decoding) modules in the same block in order to reduce the complexity of the overall system, comparing to the tandem scheme.
