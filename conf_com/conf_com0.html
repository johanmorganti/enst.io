Abstract

Nowadays optical communication systems provide a very large proportion of telecommunication links, particarly for long-distance high-bit-rate transmissions. The object of the course is to present the various components and devices which are implicated in the design of an optical system. The principles of optical sources and modulators are studied. The optical fibre, which constitutes the most important transmission channel, is analyzed. Advanced reception techniques are reviewed. The course includes laboratory demonstrations of the components and concepts studied.

Sudents who feel particularly interested in the subject of the course and who wish to spend a longer period at the ENST are welcome to applied for Research Project Work with the Communications Department research groups or for a one-year MASTERE course : Dispositifs et Techniques de Communications.
