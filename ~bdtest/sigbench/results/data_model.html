Data Model

Data models are statistical models that simulate certain typical cartographic applications (spatial information), such as geological maps, cities maps or world maps. They are here used to produce random Data samples from given numbers and distributions of rectangles representing the Minimum Boundary Boxes around cartographic objects.

Data models will also be used to a posteriori estimate the statistical parameters extracted from real life samples.

Data model = (Type, Underlying map definition, Number of rectangles, X distribution, Y distribution, Area Distribution, Angle distribution, Coverage )
where
Type is [Statistical model | Real life data]
Underlying map definition is (Xmin, Xmax, Ymin, Ymax)
[X|Y|Area|Angle] Distribution is (Law, Min value, Max value, Median, Variance)
Coverage is Sum of the Area of rectangles / Area of map
or Number of rectangles * Average of the Area of Rectangles / Area of map
