Learning to Communicate

Communication, Sociology and Humanities

Joining a company means becoming part of a team, helping it meet its goals, and assuming the social--not to say--the sociable aspect of team work. ENST has designed a course of instruction that gives students the opportunity to develop skills as effective confident communicators. This is a key component of their future personal and professional lives.

This preparation includes diagnostic exercises for oral and written communications. Internships are analyzed in terms of the organizational framework and the student's personal experience. A number of seminars are dedicated to the basic techniques of expression and communication as well as to the organizational contexts and the possibilities of individual initiative.

For those who wish to broaden their training by an analysis of contemporary political, social and cultural contexts, there are courses in history and geopolitics that are offered, as well as philosophy, psychoanalysis or the history of art. The Department of Humanities has focused its research activities on communication between individuals. In particular, it studies the ways of teaching communication and management, the models of personal communication, the situations and strategies of communication and the relations between writing and computing.
