Welcome to the PI page.

These digits are the 100,000 first decimals of PI that my program found.
They were computed on a DEC ALPHA with OPEN VMS in May 1994.
My program was written in Pascal for VMS.

I used the Machin's formula: Pi/4 = 4.Arctg(1/5) - Arctg(1/239)
and the development:
arctg(x) = x - x^3/3 + x^5/5 - x^7/7 + x^9/9 ....


The digits:
from 1 to 100,000
