Title : TREATMENT AND FUSION OF MULTISOURCE IMAGE DATA

Abstract :

The principal objective of this work is to apply the Decision and Data Fusion theories on image data obtained from different origins, in order to validate antique cadastral restitutions. A method to determine the voluntary, regular and orthonormal division of principally agricultural land into uniform squares during the roman era will be established. We aim to evaluate the relevance of the hypotheses already proposed, their exactitude, and to put forward an approach adapted for our application, based on fusion of multisource data.
