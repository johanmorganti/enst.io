One Week course on

Optical Communications

23 - 28 February, 1997



Course content

Five 3-hour lectures :
  • System design and performance, coherent optical systems. Demonstration on a submarine telecommunication terminal at the ENST.
  • External modulators and integrated optic devices (integrated optical waveguides, electro-optic and electro-absorption effects).
  • Sources for optical communications : LED and laser-diodes. General operation and properties of devices (LED, Fabry-Perot and DFB cavities, homo and heterojunction, quantum well lasers). Modulation and noise properties.
  • Detection of optical radiation, Photodetectors (pin and avalanche detectors, noise sources effecting detection).
  • Optical fibres (guiding, attenuation, dispersion properties).
Five associated lab sessions :

Characterization of optical fibres (attenuation and dispersion measurements), characterization of laser-diodes, electro-optic modulators and integrated optics, characterization of photodetectors and observation of receiver noise, demonstration of a heterodyne detection system.
