Research

    Semiconductors, Computers, Telecommunications

  • Management of Information Systems

  • Organization Theory

  • Productivity of Information Technology

  • Economics of Organizations

  • Development of Statistical Tools and realization of Databases on Information Industries

  • Marketing of New Products and Services

  • International Industrial Economics and Inter-Firm Cooperation in Information Technology

  • Economics of European Integration

  • Spectrum Management Analysis

  • Innovation and the Economics of Property Rights

  • Innovation and Small Business Economics :
    SMEs Growth, Firm Performance and size, Investment and Capital Diversity in the Small Enterprise, Technology, strategy and flexibility of SMEs, Information Technology and SMEs organizational design, Information Technology and competitive advantages of SMEs, SMEs Informatization, Innovation, risk and SMEs Financing.
