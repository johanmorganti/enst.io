Places you have visited / Your Language


Ask your partner these questions. He will do the same in French.

a)

Where were you born?

Does your family still live there?

Have you always lived in the same house?

Would you like to live in a different part of the country? Why/why not?

Where are your parents from?

Does all your family, (cousins etc) live close together?

Can you describe where your home-town is?

Is it far from London, Sheffield?

Have you been to any other countries?

Which do you prefer and why?

Is there a difference between the north and south of your country?

What do you think are the main differences between France and England?

Have you ever been to France? Where/when/why?

What did you like most?


b)

Are there any regional differences?

Are there any dialects?

Does the accent/grammar differ much?

What kind of language do you hear on television/radio?

Which accents/dialects are considered the most prestigious?

Do your grandparents use the same kind of language as you?
