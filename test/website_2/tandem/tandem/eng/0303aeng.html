Home sweet home

Ask your partner to tell you something about her /his hometown and county.

  • it history
  • its landscape
  • customs and peculiarities
  • its people
  • its importance for the area/ for the country
  • its attractions
  • the cultural facilities it offers

Take notes (in English of course) which will enable you to tell your classmates back home about the area you have been to in Ireland.
