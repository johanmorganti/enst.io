There's no accounting for taste

We all know that but we are assuming that each of you would like to know what his or her partner likes.

Tasks

Below we have provided a series of subjects you can ask each other about using the following questions:

  • Do you like it? Why (not)?
  • What does it remind you of?
  • What do you associate it with?
  • ...
