Speaking without words

In every language it takes more that words to communicate. Mime and gesture play an important part.

Whether you smile, turn up your nose or frown – these signals are always correctly interpreted.

The same is true with gestures: pulling your tongue out to someone, winking or bowing are also signs that have a clear meaning. Isn't that so?
