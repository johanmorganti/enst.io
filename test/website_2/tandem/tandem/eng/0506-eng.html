TV survey

0506-eng [RTF] – [auf deutsch] [in English] [en español] [en français] [em português] [på svenska] (15.02.97)

Task 1

Read the following questions with your partner and note down his/her answers in English.

  • How many hours a day do you spend in front of the box?

  • What programmes do you enjoy watching?

  • What type of programmes do you avoid?

  • What criteria do you use for deciding what you watch?

  • To what extent are you influenced by advertising?

  • As a family or amongst friends, how do you resolve the problem of conflicting interests when each wants to watch a different programme?
