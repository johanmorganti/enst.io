Studying in England

0402-eng [RTF] – [auf deutsch] [in English] [en español] [en français] [em português] [på svenska] (15.02.97)

Since the pattern of study and lectures differ a great deal from one country to another, we think it will be useful for you to give each other information on the subject, in such a way that you will be able to describe it at a later date.

Tasks

  • Fill in the timetable below with that of your partner.
  • Ask about the lectures and studies. What do the lecturers do? And the students? What is the content of the lectures like?
  • Ask for an explanation of the relative importance of each course and subject. (What about qualifications? exams? Are lectures, etc. compulsory? ... )
  • Find out whether, apart from lectures, your partner has to study at home. (What and for how long? ... )
