One Week course on

Optical Communications

23 - 28 February, 1997



Practical Information

  • Programme February 23 : Arrival and housing of student participants. Registration and Welcome planned : 16:00-18:00 at the Maison des Elèves at 212, rue de Tolbiac, Paris 75013. Telephone : 33 1 45 89 00 63.

  • February 23-march 1 : Housing is available for participating students until the morning of Morning Sunday, March 2.

Participating students should bring sleeping bags. During the week, The ENST will provide for lunch. Evening meals at the Student Cantine will cost approximately 15 francs per meal. Participants will need about 100-200 francs per day for breakfast and sundry expenses. Those who stay through Saturday, March 1, should count an extra 150-200 francs for meals that day.

  • February 24 : Course on Optical Communications begins (Details on the final programme will be provided at a later date). Student participants should meet with course organizer, Didier Erasme, at 8:30 in the main hall of ENST, 46, rue Barrault.
Each evening of the week a special social programme will be organized by ENST students and/or the Administration. Part of the afternoon of Wednesday, February 25, will be reserved for a "Language Workshop" between participants and ENST students. Details on these activities will follow at a later date.



Public Transportation :

Ecole Nationale Supérieure des Télécommunications and the student residence are situated close to the Paris Metro : "Corvisart" on the Nation-Etoile Line. See WWW map.
