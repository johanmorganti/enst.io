Résumé :

La presence de satellite dans les futures reseaux large bande est incountournable. Il aura un important rôle à jouer dans le contexte de communications multimédia interactive. Le sujet de la these est concerne par la definition d'une architecture du reseau satellitaire qui peut etre adaptee a offrir les futures services multimedia. Les protocoles d'acces multiple au canal radio constitue un aspect tres important dans l'etude. Les reseaux VSAT actuelle sont etudies pour arriver a une solution optimale afin de les adapter aux services requis.

Title : Satellite System Architectures Adapted to Multimedia Services

Abstract :

Satellites play an important role in the rapidly evolving multimedia era. Satellite networks have to be redesigned in such a way to be able to provide offered multimedia services to a large individual user population. The thesis work is concerned with defining a satellite network architecture in this context, focusing on the development of a multi-access protocol that can allow a large number of users to share the satellite transponder. The VSAT networks represent a good candidate the can be adapted to evolving multimedia applications.
