Title : Median Linear Orders in Tournaments

Abstract :

A tournament is a complete antisymmetric oriented graph. In this thesis, we deal with the following problem : given a tournament, what is the minimum number of arcs that must be reversed in order to make the tournament transitive ? Such a transitive tournament represents a linear order, called a median order. Finding one or all the median orders of a tournament is a hard problem, and the algorithms nowadays known to solve it are exponential. Then it is necessary to find, by the means of theoritical considerations, some properties of the median orders, so as to introduce them in the design of algorithms computing them. These studies lead also to the characterization of classes of tournaments easier to solve with a computer.
