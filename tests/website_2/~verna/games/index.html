Hypermine !!
Hypermine is a real HTML on-line mine game !!
If you don't know the rules, here they are.


Monsieur et Madame ont un fils !!
Reserved to people speaking French, I give you the familiy name, and you give me the first name. An example I found in English (I'm very proud of it ;-): Mr. and Mss. Herchief have a son ... Hank !! (hankerchief). I would be interested in building a database of such jokes in English as I did in French. If you have any propositions, tell me.

WebMaze !!
Incredible !! WebMaze is an automatic VRML maze generator !! You specify certain parameters to give to the maze, and WebMaze throws back the maze directly to your 3d browser.
If you don't have any 3d browser, throw an eye to this page.
