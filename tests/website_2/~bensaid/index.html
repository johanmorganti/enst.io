The AJGE was created in 1984 from the need to gather Jewish students scattered in the different campus of Business Schools & Engineer Schools of France in order to enable them to preserve their identity, to develop their knowledge of Judaism and reinforce their belonging to the Jewish Community.

In this aim, the AJGE proposes very different activities in order to interest a large public made of hundreds of students from Engineer & Business Schools and Universities.
