Mediatheque : Language Resource Center

The Language Resource Center, coordinated by Silvana Taillefer, is in the Documentation Centre (B707):

  • A videotape collection
  • A language laboratory
  • An audio-cassette lending service
  • A multimedia PC and CD-ROM collection
  • Books
  • Foreign newspapers

Multimedia PC's and an up-to-date CD-ROM Collection

An audio-cassette lending service

Books

All books in foreign language can be borrowed: methods, literature, comic books... Dictionaries and other reference books can be consulted in the center.

  • Authentik : German, English, Spanish, French as a foreign language
