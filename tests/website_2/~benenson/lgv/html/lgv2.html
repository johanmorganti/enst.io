Pedagogy / Methods

  • Pedagogical techniques
  • Goals
    • To communicate orally and in writing in a foreign language in various situations and contexts.
    • To acquire learning skills adapted to each student for individual or collective work.
    • To become familiar with the civilizations of the languagesstudied by understanding the differences between that culture and the students' native culture.
    • To acquire the linguistic tools essential for an engineer.

    Pedagogical techniques

    • Interactive approach during courses
    • Work in small language classes (8 to 14 students), small groups in a class and personalized teaching (student counseling, tutoring and individual projects)
    • Intensive sessions (week-end workshops) And bilingual activities (intensive workgroups in German/English, face-to-face tandems and e-mail tandems)
    • Theatre and singing in English and Spanish
    • Individual linguistic projects in a foreign country
    • Teaching programs adapted to students' needs and desires: courses based on purely linguistic work, on particular skills, theme courses...)
