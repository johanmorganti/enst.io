Is there a basic difference between a German and an Irish family ?

Describe a typical German family and tell your partner how you see a typical Irish family. (You may take your own family as an example)

Take the following aspects into consideration:

  • size of the family (parents, children, other members ?)
  • daily life
  • family income and expenses
  • chores and who does them
  • freetime activities - individual ones and those involving the whole family
