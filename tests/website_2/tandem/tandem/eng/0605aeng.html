Tales

Tales and legends are part of the cultural heritage of a people. Didn't we all love to listen to them, especially on stormy and cold winter evenings.
Do you remember asking your parents to read you a story before going asleep when you were little ?

Please ask your partner to tell you in English a story he or she remembers from her /his childhood days. If your partner can't think of one, ask your guest parents or friends for help.
Write down the outline of the story.
