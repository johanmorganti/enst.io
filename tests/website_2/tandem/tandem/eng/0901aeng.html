Cursing

Sometimes it's handy to at least understand some examples of abusive language.
One does not always have to use it oneself.

Ask your partner to explain the following expressions and to give you an example of situations in which they might be used.

Silly cow
Bloody eejit
Son of a bitch
Silly bitch
"Jaysus"
Bullocks
Kiss my arse
