Holidays and celebrations



Our life is punctuated by holidays and celebrations of all kinds.Every country and every region has its own special days in addition to holidays which we may share in common.Here are the most important celebrations and holidays in Britain:


  • New years Day (January 1st)
  • Hogmanay
  • Mothering Sunday (March 26th)
  • April Fools' Day (April 1st)
  • Good Friday (Friday before Easter Sunday)
  • Palm Sunday
  • Easter Sunday
  • Easter Monday
  • May Day Holiday
  • Spring Bank Holiday (the last Monday of May)
  • Summer Bank Holiday (the last Monday of August)
  • Christmas Day (December 25th)
  • Boxing Day (December 26th)
  • Guy Fawkes Night (November 5th)
  • Shrove Tuesday
  • St Valentine Day
  • Halloween
