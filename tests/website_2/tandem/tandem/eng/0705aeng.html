Poetry



Flat. Lifeless.
rain soaked green
sprouts reluctantly
into isolated trees,
staring at a blank landscape,
wistfully watching
fleeing clouds.
Alone with daylight
silent hopeless
waiting for night.
Evelyn Conlon




Task: Read out the poem and ask your partner to explain words you don't understand.

Discuss with your partner in English whether or not the poem gives a proper impression of Maynooth. What would you say is correct ? What do you think is different ?

If you like you might try to write your own poem about Maynooth - with the help of your partner of course. You might also try to take a photograph or draw a picture which illustrates either the mood of the poem or your own feelings about Maynooth.
