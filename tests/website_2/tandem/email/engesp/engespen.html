ENG-ESP

Spanish-English subnet in the International E-Mail Tandem Network

[en español] (22.06.97)

If you speak perfect Spanish and are learning English, or the other way around, then this is the right subnet for you.

How you can participate

  • First of all, apply to the central Tandem Agency. Write to the following
    address:
    tandem@slf.ruhr-uni-bochum.de
    the following message: "ENG-ESP. My native language is English. I'm learning Spanish. My name is...." As soon as you get a partner, you can correspond with him or her.
  • Apply to the Forum ENG-ESP, in which you can discuss all kinds of topics with others in English and Spanish. Write to the following address:
    majordomo@tandem.uni-trier.de
    the following message (without quotation marks): "SUBSCRIBE ENG-ESP"
  • After your application has been processed, introduce yourself in the forum. Send a short letter about yourself to the forum address
    eng-esp@tandem.uni-trier.de
    – preferably in both languages.

Practical hints

Through the forum ENG-ESP, the Tandem Dating Agency, and here you will find further practical hints concerning tandem learning with your partner and participation in the forum.
