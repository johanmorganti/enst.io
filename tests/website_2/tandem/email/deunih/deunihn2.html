DEU-NIH ni youkoso!

DEU-NIH ha doitsu to nihon no disukasshon fooramu desu. Kono fooramu ha, doitsugo ka nihongo no dekiru hito ga, doitsugo ya nihongo wo benkyou suruba desu. Sara ni, doitsu, arui ha nihon no paatonaa ni tsuite shiritai hitotachi no deai no ba demo arimasu.

Kono fooramu de ha, arayuru koto wo kaku koto ga dekimasu. Infomeeshon wo motomeru koto mo dekimasu shi, jibun kara infomeeshon ya iken wo dasu koto mo dekimasu. Jibun no kojintekina taiken wo houkoku suru koto mo dekimasu. Bogo demo, gaikokugo demo, arui ha 2-ka-kokugo de kaku koto mo dekimasu. Taisetsuna no ha, doku-nichi-ryou-gaikokugo de kakareta messeeji no kazu ga, daitai tsuriau youni suru koto desu. Kaiin mina ga, gaikokugo de yondari, kaitari suru chansu wo, onaji dake moteru youni suru tame desu.

Tandemu paatonaa to tsuushin sureba, motto takusan no koto wo manabu koto ga dekimasu. "Tandem" toha, kotonatta bunka de seikatsu shite iru futari no ningen ga, o-tagai ni machigai wo naoshitari, jogen wo ataetari, shitsumon wo shiattari suru kotoga dekimasu.

Doitsugo ga bogo de, nihongo wo manabou to shite iru kata, mata, sono gyaku no kata ni totte, "doku-nichi sabunetto" ha saitekina sabunetto desu.
