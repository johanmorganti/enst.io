CLASSICAL MUSIC

EL PARAIGUA
Pas de l'ensenyança, 2. Pl. Sant Jaume.

This historical bar offers you a modernist decorated café from 1902 or, downstairs, a perfect place to relax while listening to classical music.

CAFE

IL CAFE DI ROMA
Via Laietana, 44 or Via Laietana/Pl. de l'Àngel

Two places to enjoy the real Italian espresso and cappuccino. Also lots of varieties of teas... And don't forget to try their genuine Italian grappa!

JAZZ CLUB

HARLEM
Comtessa de Sobradiel, 8

The fashionable jazz-club in Barcelona. From Tuesday till Sunday, jazz live. No cover charge.

JUZZ CLUB

JAMBOREE
Pl. Reial, 17

Small and crowed disco. Jazz, acid-jazz, and other live music concerts.

TAVERNS (TASCAS)

TASCA LA MUSIQUETA
Avinyó, 31

The perfect place to sing, have fun, make friends, etc. Listen to the music you enjoy, have 800 ptas/litter beer and tapas, pinchos or bravas.

TAVERN / TABERNA

PARNASSE
Gignàs, 21

Apollonian tavern. Jazz, Brassens, puppets, movies, live music. French and Greek food specialities.

CAFE BAR

CAFÈ DE L'ÒPERA
La Rambla, 74

The authentic atmosphere of the turn-of-the-century's Rambla, for coffee, tea, tapas, beers or national and imported wines.

LIVE MUSIC

BAR PASTÍS
Sta. Mònica, 4

With almost half a century of existence, this little bar with a real French atmosphere, is one of the most distinctive of Barcelona. French music "chansons".
