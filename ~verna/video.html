Live from my office ...



If everything goes well, you will get a picture taken live from my office. If you click on it, you will get a 100 frames Mpeg movie.
Since one people only may access the video at a time, you might get a "busy" instead of an image.
Since I use the camera in my work, it's not always available. In that case, you may receive a server error. Don't complain to my sys adm !! I don't want to break my friendship with him ;-} ...


The cgi used to provide the video is called Webcam.
