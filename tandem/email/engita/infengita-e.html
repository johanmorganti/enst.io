If you speak perfect English and are learning Italian, or the other way around, then this is the right subnet for you.

How you can participate

  • First of all, apply to the central Tandem Agency. Write to the following address:
    tandem@slf.ruhr-uni-bochum.de
    Your message could be, for example: "Subnet ENG-ITA. I am a professional / college student / high school student. My native language is English. I'm learning Italian. My name is...." As soon as you get a partner, you can start corresponding with each other.
  • Apply to the Forum ENG-ITA, in which you can discuss all kinds of topics with others in English and Italian. Write to the following address:
    majordomo@tandem.uni-trier.de
    the following message (without quotation marks): "SUBSCRIBE ENG-ITA"
  • After your application has been processed, introduce yourself in the forum. Send a short letter about yourself to the forum address
    ENG-ITA@tandem.uni-trier.de
    – preferably in both languages.

Practical hints

Through the forum ENG-ITA, the Tandem Agency, and here you will find further practical hints concerning tandem learning with your partner and participation in the forum.
