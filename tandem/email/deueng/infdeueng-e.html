If you speak perfect German and are learning English, or the other way around, then this is the right subnet for you. - RIBO-L was founded by the University of Rhode Island (USA) and the Ruhr-University BOchum (Germany) in 1992 – thus the name.

How you can participate

  • First of all, apply to the central Tandem Agency. Write to the following address:
    tandem@slf.ruhr-uni-bochum.de
    Your message could be, for example: "Subnet RIBO-L. I am a professional / college student / high school student. My native language is English. I'm learning German. My name is...." As soon as you get a partner, you can start corresponding with each other.
  • Apply to the Forum RIBO-L, in which you can discuss all kinds of topics with others in English and German. Write to the following address:
    listserv@uriacc.uri.edu
    the following message (without quotation marks): "SUBSCRIBE RIBO-L First name Last name"
  • After your application has been processed, introduce yourself in the forum. Send a short letter about yourself to the forum address
    RIBO-L@uriacc.uri.edu
    – preferably in both languages.

Practical hints

Through the forum RIBO-L, the Tandem Agency, and here you will find further practical hints concerning tandem learning with your partner and participation in the forum.
