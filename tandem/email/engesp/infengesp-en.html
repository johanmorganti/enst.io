If you speak perfect English and are learning Spanish, or the other way around, then this is the right subnet for you.

How you can participate

  • First of all, apply to the central Tandem Agency. Write to the following address:
    tandem@slf.ruhr-uni-bochum.de
    Your message could be, for example: "Subnet ENG-ESP. I am a professional / college student / high school student. My native language is English. I'm learning Spanish. My name is...." As soon as you get a partner, you can start corresponding with each other.
  • Apply to the Forum ENG-ESP, in which you can discuss all kinds of topics with others in English and Spanish. Write to the following address:
    majordomo@tandem.uni-trier.de
    the following message: SUBSCRIBE ENG-ESP
  • After your application has been processed, introduce yourself in the forum. Send a short letter about yourself to the forum address
    ENG-ESP@tandem.uni-trier.de
    – preferably in both languages.

How to unsubscribe

To unsubscribe from ENG-ESP, if you are, for example, on holiday or if you want to leave the forum completely:

  • Send the message: UNSUBSCRIBE ENG-ESP
    to this address:     majordomo@tandem.uni-trier.de

To subscribe to ENG-ESP again, just repeat the subscription process explained above.

Practical hints

Through the forum ENG-ESP, the Tandem Agency, and here you will find further practical hints concerning tandem learning with your partner and participation in the forum.

MundoHispano

An idea to start out with! You and your partner can do more than just write each other letters - you can meet every once in a while in the

"Café Tándem" in MundoHispano

MundoHispano is a MOO, a virtual reality, where people from Spain and Latin America often meet Spanish learners.

Have fun!
