What would you do ?

Imagine you had children. What would you do if....

First fill in the questionnaire for yourself. Then ask your partner what he/she would do in the given situation. Either do this exercise in one language only or change after about half the questions. Discuss your answers as detailed as possible. No matter which language you choose for the discussion, the notes you take afterwards are always written in the target language.
