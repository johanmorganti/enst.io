Holiday of your dreams

0106-eng [RTF] – [auf deutsch] [in English] [en español] [en français] [em português] [på svenska] (15.02.97)

Tasks

  • Imagine that the world is your oister: a millionaire's bank balance, all the time in the world, choice of company ... -- how would you spend your holidays?
    Of course, it may be that you have already experienced an unforgettable holiday. What was it like?
    Describe to your partner in your own language the journey, the place, the people, the atmosphere -- in short, all that strikes you as important. Don't be afraid of fantasising, of exaggerating.
    Your partner will take notes accordingly, and you will do likewise when he/she is talking in English about his/her dream holiday.

  • It is possible that your dreams coincide, but also that they may be totally different. However it turns out, you've now got to plan your holiday together -- ideal or real -- so that both of you can get the best out of it.
