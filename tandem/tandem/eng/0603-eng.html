Task

Since time immemorial man has enjoyed conjuring up an imaginary world very different from the real world, a world with roles reversed, in which other physical laws, etc. operate. It could be, however, that this topsy--turvy world is in many cases the right way round, don't you think?

Write out something similar with your partners: in the form of poetry, or a list of wishes (Wouldn't it be marvellous if ... ), or in the form of an amusing tale (Once upon a time ...), because we're sure you will have better ideas.
