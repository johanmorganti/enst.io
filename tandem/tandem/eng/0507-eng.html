The right person for the job

0507-eng [RTF] – [auf deutsch] [in English] [en español] [en français] [em português] [på svenska] (15.02.97)

Task

You are looking to fill the post of animateur-in-chief at a prestigious holiday club, the Don Valley Spa Club. The following have applied for the post:

  1. Ms. María A., Spanish, 26 years old, unmarried with a child of 3, air hostess, fluent in Spanish, English and German.

  2. Mrs. Susanne B., German, 37 years old, divorced, without children, sports teacher, fluent in German, English, French and with a limited knowledge of Spanish;

  3. Mr. Philip C., English, 34 years old, married, with two young sons, graduate in Tourism, fluent in English, Spanish and French;

  4. Mr. Henri D., French, 30 years old, separated, tour guide, fluent in French, English, German, Spanish, Italian and Suaheli.

You and your partner have received these four applications with all the documentation and have opted for one or other of the applicants. Discuss your choice with your partner and try to come to decision as to the most suitable. Note down your reasons and be prepared to defend your choice in front of the whole group.
