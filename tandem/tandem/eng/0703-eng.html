An eye for an eye

Task

Collect some English expressions or proverbs with your partner which are connected

  • with animals (i.e. wolf, sheep, lamb, bull, dog, cat, horse) or
  • with parts of the body (i.e. toe, foot, leg, arm, head ).

Note down not only the expressions but also their meaning and the equivalent expressions in the other language.
