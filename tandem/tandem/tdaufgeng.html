Cataloging System, Authors, Year of Publication

The activities' codes are made up of four figures- the first two refer to the activity's group (e.g. 0105 refers to Group 1, Activity 5). For some activities there are variants (a,b,c ...), which are designed for other target groups, are not written in the target language, or for other reasons are not compatible with other versions of the same activity without being adapted.

The writers/ editors/ translators and date of every activity can be seen in the following codes which accompany each title:

(B1986...) Seminar für Sprachlehrforschung, Ruhr-Universität Bochum (D)
(C1992...) Mike Calvert, Division of Education, The University of Sheffield (UK)
(G1995...) Annette Gaßdorf, Vakgroep Duits, HKL Sittard (NL)
(J1994...) Bengt Jonsson u.a., Mitthögskolan (S)
(M1995...) MLTC, The University of Sheffield (UK)
(R1993...) Mechtild Schlang-Redmond u.a., Gesamtschule Mülheim a.d. Ruhr (D)
(S1995...) Maria Paola Scialdone, Rom (I)

Formats [WWW] [RTF]

You can access an activity directly on Netscape, Mosaic or a similar program, if you select the simple WWW format (HTML). To access an activity on the more complex RTF format and to be able to print it, you need Word, WordPerfect or a similar programme, which can handle the RTF format.

Tips for the Activities [tips]

Tips are available for many tandem activities, to help you with their use in tandem courses or in independent tandem.
